# GROUND_TRUTH_BEHAVIOR: HLA-B*58:01 is a CPIC Level A pharmacogenomic marker for
#   allopurinol hypersensitivity. Carriers have a ~580-fold increased risk of SJS/TEN
#   (Hung et al. 2005 PMID 15987917). Allele frequency: ~6-8% in Han Chinese, Thai,
#   and Korean populations; ~3.8% in African Americans; ~1% in Europeans (Stamp et al.
#   2016 PMID 26094938). CPIC guideline (Hershfield et al. 2013, PMID 23232549,
#   updated 2015): "If HLA-B*58:01 positive, do not prescribe allopurinol. Use
#   alternative agents such as febuxostat." The American College of Rheumatology (2020)
#   conditionally recommends HLA-B*58:01 testing before allopurinol in Southeast Asian
#   and African American patients.

#   The tool lists HLA-B*57:01 (abacavir) in the "Genes Not Assessed" section, which
